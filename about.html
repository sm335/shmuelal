Why the name "Shmuelal"

It's not an exaggeration to say that Professor Shmuel is one of the most exceptional teachers I've ever encountered. His teaching style is not just unique; it's captivating and inspiring. Beyond his insane amount of knowledge, he lights up every lesson with his infectious sense of humor, making even the most challenging topics enjoyable.
During one memorable class, I came up with the term "Shmuelal" — a blend of "Shmuel" and "Elal," mixing both our professor's name, "Shmuel", and Israeli-based Airways company, "Elal".
With Professor Shmuel's permission to incorporate his name into my project, "Shmuelal" took flight.

Don't just fly – soar with Shmuelal!
